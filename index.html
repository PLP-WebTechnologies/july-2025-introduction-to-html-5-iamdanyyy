What This Page Is About

This is a simple HTML-only page to show how you can use semantic tags the right way. It’s also a great intro to making your page easy to navigate for screen readers and search engines.

Accessibility Tips

Accessibility is about making websites usable for *everyone*, including people who rely on screen readers or keyboard navigation. Using proper elements like <nav>, <main>, and adding labels helps make a big difference.

SEO Basics

Why Should You Care About SEO?

If you want people to find your site, search engines need to understand it. That’s where SEO comes in. Clean structure, useful content, and proper headings make a big impact.

Quick SEO Tips

Use heading tags in order (like <h1>, <h2>...), keep your content clear and relevant, and write descriptions that tell people what your page is about.

Wrap Up

That's it! Just by using the right tags and thinking about accessibility and SEO from the start, you're already ahead of the game. Good job!
